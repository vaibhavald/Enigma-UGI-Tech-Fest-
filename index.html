About Us?

Enigma is United Group of Institution’s biggest cultural & tech-fest and has established itself as a legacy where everyone looks forward to coming together be it, competing or enjoying. Offering a rich blend of music art, dance, technical and numerous other fun events.

A year’s worth of excitement, fervor and hard work packed into 2 amazing days. More than 17 colleges come to participate in this techno-cultural fest. There are total 30 events which gives each participant a new experience and learning followed by a celebrity night. Our vision is to serve as a platform for those aspiring to turn their vision into tangible reality.

So, buckle up and get ready for the most thrilling night ever where you get drenched in the adrenaline rush.

BATTLE OF BREADBOARDS

This is an technical event which involves the skills to perform and execute the theoretical laws practically, using analog devices and components. As the name suggests, it is an event which comprises of knowledge about circuit making on the breadboard along with the knowledge of basic analog electronic components. The event comprises of 3 rounds.

The event comprises of 3 rounds.
  • Round-1
    It is an Elimination Round where each team would be given M.C.Q’ s. Fixed no. of teams with the best scores would advance to the second round.
  • Round-2
    The teams would be given a circuit and required to verify Kirchoff’s law practically as well as theoretically and match the values. Fixed no. of teams with the lowest clocked time would advance to the 3rd round.
  • Round-3
    • It is the circuit making on the breadboard.
  • FOR 1st YEAR
    1. Teams would have to design a rectifier circuit on breadboard
  • FOR 2nd ,3rd YEAR
      Teams would have to design any one of the given circuit.
    1. 555 timer circuit.
    2. LED chaser circuit.
    3. Any innovative circuit design ( teams would have to inform before 16 november about circuit)
Rules
  1. Each team would comprise of at most 2 members.
  2. Further information about each round would be provided to the participants on the day of the event.
  3. Teams are allowed to bring scientific calculator for calculations.
  4. The rules are subjected to change without prior notice.
  5. The decision of the Event Manager would be considered to be final.
  6. The participants are requested to make their registration before the start of the event.
  7. Breadboard and other elements will be provided.
